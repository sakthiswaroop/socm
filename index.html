Who we are

Stories of Common Man

Common people are everywhere. We as a group of volunteers present all over India spare a few minutes to ask them about their story. Some don’t want to get published, some ask for money as they are starving, others hope that something will happen after they meet us. But, most of the time, they just hold our hands with love, offer us tea or food and appreciate our efforts. We leave an impact by helping them in a way where they can face their problems, find a solution and evolve themselves. Also, many have come forward to help these people. So far, we have helped 82 people.
